Mandarin. Live it. A truly immersive experince.

Chinese is one of the world's most difficult major languages. That's why we provide an immersive experince that engages all your senses and reinforces your understanding of Mandarin. Once you try China's delicious food or walk the peculiar streets, you're sure to remember everything that goes along with it.

Exceptional Teachers. They understand how you learn.

All of our instuctors are certified to teach Chinese at the university level. Many of them have over 10 years of experince. Chinese is tough and you'll want the best help you can get. If your just starting or studying for HSK 6, our teacher have the knowledge and patience to get you where you want to go.

Learn with friends from around the world. You're never alone.

You'll be placed in a small group with people who match your current Chinese abilities. During your time with us, you cohort will take lessons together, schedule study sessions, and even travel to neighboring cities. Learning Chinese with others creates a supportive learning environment and encourages everyone to start speaking in Mandarin from day one.
